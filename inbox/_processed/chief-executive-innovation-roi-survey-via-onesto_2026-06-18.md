## Figures (line-checked against the primary)
- 58% of CEOs: <10% of revenue from offerings not sold 5 years ago; only 22% have ≥20% from new offerings; 43% entered new areas with "limited impact on revenue."
- 77% say expansion into new categories/markets/offerings will be critical/important over 3–5 yrs (~30% "critical").
- R&D: average 8.8% of net revenue (2025), 9.4% forecast (2026); up from 7% (2023), 6% (2024); median 5%.
- Ownership: PE-backed — ~1/3 already significant, 88% critical/important; family-owned lower on both.
- Sectors: healthcare 0% significant / 86% critical-important; industrial manufacturing 31% significant (vs 22% avg); tech/software/telecom high R&D + more likely ≥20% from new offerings; construction/mining <10% from new products.
